What Information we collect and how we use your information

We collect various types of information in connection with the Service, including:

1. Device & app history

Retrieve running apps
We use this information to improve the basic quality of the HOLA Launcher to ensure that it is fast, stable in user’s phone. For example, you tap HOLA Boost to clean your device's RAM.

2. Identity

Find accounts on the device
We do not recognize and save your account. We only detect whether you have a Google account in the phone. This helps us to confirm the state of Google Service and provide users with appropriate application update methods.

3. Contacts

Read your contacts
We do not save or upload your contacts. We just use this permission to read your contacts when you initiate contact searching, and display possible results.

4. SMS

Read your text messages (SMS and MMS)
We do not save or upload your messages. We just use this permission to read your messages when you initiate message searching, and display possible results.

5. Location

Approximate location (network-based)
We do not locate your detailed location information. The only purpose using this permission is to display apps that surrounding users installed anonymously, and recommend popular apps based on current location in HOLA Radar. This information can’t be traced back.

6. Camera

Take pictures and videos
Hola Shine tools is a popular feature in HOLA. We developed it by controlling the camera flash lamp of phone. This requires the camera permission. HOLA do not take photos and videos by itself.

7. Photos ⁄ Media ⁄ Files

Modify or delete the contents of your USB storage
Test access to protected storage
We use this permission to launch the apps installed in the SD card and store the wallpaper. They are basic features for an android launcher app.

8. Wi-Fi connection information

View Wi-Fi connections
We use this permission to control Wi-Fi switch. So you can be easy to turn on and off the WLAN.

9. Device ID & call information

Read phone status and identity
We use this information to evaluate the effects of our various promotion methods and improve our service. E.g. determine which language should be supported in HOLA Launcher.

10. Calendar

Read calendar events plus confidential information
We use the first half of this permission to read your calendar events so that we can display and remind you of your coming events on the wind chime widget. The second half? No, we never read your confidential information without your consent.
