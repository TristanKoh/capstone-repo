Solo Launcher Privacy Policy and Terms of Service

This Privacy Notice describes the types of personal information we collect about our users and how we may use the information.
We want to emphasize that the only purpose of accessing this information is to make the functions listed below work, and any information besides basic anonymous Solo Launcher usage data will always keep in your cellphone.

    Device & App History

  • Read sensitive log data
  • Retrieve running apps

We use this information to improve the basic quality of the Solo Launcher to ensure that it is fast, stable in users’ phones. For example, by constantly analyzing this information, we have reduced the crash rate to only 0.07%.

    Identity

  • Find accounts on the device

We do not recognize and save your account. We only detect whether you have a Google account in the phone or not. This helps us to confirm the state of Google Service and provide users with appropriate application update methods.

    Contacts & Phone

  • Directly call phone numbers
  • Read your contact

The permission is for creating shortcut on the home screen, and users can check and dial easily which will save their time and unnecessary clicks.

    Photos ⁄ Media ⁄ Files

  • Modify or delete the contents of your USB storage
  • Test access to protected storage

We use this permission to launch the apps installed in the SD card and store the wallpaper. They are basic features for an android launcher app.

    Approximate location (network-based)

The permission is for Solo Weather widget to locate your city and provide the accurate weather information for you.

    Camera

  • Take pictures and videos

Torch is a popular feature in Solo Launcher. We developed it by controlling the camera flashlight of phone. This requires the camera permission. Solo Launcher does not take photos or videos by itself.

    Wi-Fi connection information

  • View Wi-Fi connections

The permission is for controlling Wi-Fi switch, so that you can easily turn on and off the WLAN.

    Device ID & call information

  • Read phone status and identity

We use this information to evaluate the effects of our various promotion methods and improve our service. E.g. determine which language should be supported in Solo Launcher.
