Tune Me — Privacy Policy

We only collect anonymous usage data, with one exception: if you provide us your email address using the “Contact Us” form, we store it so we can contact you, but won't share it. This document describes our privacy practices in detail.

Information We Collect

Your account: we store a hash (digital fingerprint) of your Google account email address(es) to identify you and tie your account to your purchases. We do not store the actual email address(es), and cannot obtain them using the hash.

We collect anonymous usage data from every app user to monitor usage and fix bugs, such as device specifications (model, screen size, Android version, etc), the country in which you are located, timestamped usage data, and your hashed email address(es).

If you use the “Contact Us” form, you may voluntarily provide us with your actual email address and other personally identifiable information such as your name. We may use this information solely to contact you to respond to your questions and provide support.

Information Collected by Third Parties

The app may send information to the third party services listed below. All of this information is anonymous with the exception of your email address should you choose to provide it using the “Contact Us” form.

  • Google Analytics – for usage monitoring, including your anonymized IP address and timestamped usage data. View Google's Privacy Policy
  • Admob by Google – for in-app advertising, including an advertising ID and interest & demographic categories. View Google's Privacy Policy
  • HockeyApp – for crash reporting and bug fixes, including your device specifications and crash logs. View HockeyApp's Privacy Policy
  • UserVoice – for the “Contact Us” form, including your device specifications and email address. View UserVoice's Privacy Policy

Sharing of Information

With the exception of anonymous data sent to the third parties mentioned above, we do not share your personal information without your consent (including your email address, if provided), unless we are legally obligated to do so or believe it's needed to enforce our Terms of Service.

Opting Out

Our usage monitoring and crash reporting services help us to fix bugs and improve the quality of the app. However, you may opt of of these services using the “Do Not Track” option on the Settings screen of the app. If you do so, your anonymous usage data will not be sent to Google Analytics, HockeyApp, or our servers.

Notes & Changes

Tune Me is developed by Atonality Mobile, LLC, which operates in the United States. If you are using the app from outside the U.S., you still agree to the collection of your information as described in this document.

We may amend this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we do so, we will notify you by posting an announcement inside the app.

For questions and concerns, please email support@atonality.io. Thanks for using Tune Me!
