By using the Services, you confirm that you have read and consent to:
(1)the collection of your information as described in this Policy;

If you have any questions relating to the content of this Policy please contact us via our customer service.

2.The Information We Collect
Protecting your information and maintaining your trust is core to our mission. We will not sell your personal information to third parties.
The types of information we collect are mainly as follows:
(1) Information about malicious applications, files and other potential threats on your tablet, mobile phone or other applicable device and their behaviors and origins;
(2) Information about your device including device ID, apps of the third party installed on the device, manufacturer of the device, internet history and bookmarks, screen size and model of the device, the mobile network you use but not the phone number;
(3) Information we collected as you use our services, such as certain Log Data and public information;
“Log Data” is technical information that is automatically collected by us when you use our services, including: Error log and a DUMP file automatically generated when an unexpected error occurs or an application crashes; and we do not collect your personal identifiable information when you using our product, such as name, phone number, physical address; The number calling or called not including in the contact of the end user’s device and the number marked as the spam or the merchant number submitted by the end user. Please note that we do not collect any contact information in your device and we only read the contact number in your device to specify the number not in your contact;
Call Log not including the private information.
(4)Location Data. When you have enabled geographical location-based or GPS services on your device in relation to Our Services, we will collect information about your geographical location or GPS position based on the location of the device you are using to access our Services. That information helps us identify your physical location. Please note that we will not store or transfer your GPS information nor do we use such information to specify you.
We may also receive your personal contact information contained in correspondence via our web sites and communications channels. This enables us to offer user support, surveys, and similar services.
We constantly review and challenge the procedures we have in place for data handling and data security and have internal system and controls to ensure compliance.
