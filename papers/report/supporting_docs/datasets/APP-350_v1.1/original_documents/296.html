Privacy Policy

Privacy and Confidentiality of Information

PSafe Tecnologia S.A. (“PSafe”), CNPJ No. 12.501.419/0002-97 and No. 12.501.419/0001-06, by the nature of the services rendered, collects and, in some cases, reveals Personal Information (defined below) about users of its software/services and visitors to its site. This Privacy Policy describes the Personal Information that PSafe collects about its users and visitors, and what can be done with the Personal Information, in order to demonstrate its practices with respect to this important issue.

With this Privacy Policy, PSafe wants to show that the privacy of the information of its users is very important and, therefore, that it takes precautions and uses safeguards to protect all information, using the most complete and effective IT security mechanisms.

This document is an integrated part of the PSafe’s Terms of Use and License Agreement. By accepting of either of them, the user accepts the policies contained herein. So, when using any PSafe product or service, the user acknowledges that he has read and agreed to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Personal data (“Personal Information”) may be required to use some services offered by PSafe, or users may have the option of providing it, including name, home address, email address and contact telephone number. We may collect Personal Information about you from third parties and add this information to the information we have already collected from you via our services. PSafe may confirm the provided Personal Information by consulting with public authorities, specialized companies or databases. The information that PSafe obtains from these entities will be treated confidentially.

If the user elects to activate the anti-theft functions in PSafe Total and/or PSafe DFNDR, (i) PSafe will collect certain of the user’s Google account information (to the extent that such user has included such information in their Google account and that such information is transferred by Google to PSafe upon activation with such Google account) and (ii) PSafe may access and/or store the GPS location (of the device on which anti-theft was activated) for purposes of providing the anti-theft functionality.

PSafe automatically collects and stores some information about user activity and also about the visitors to its website. Such information may include (without limitation) any activities the user carries out with PSafe’s products or services, the URL from which the user came, the URL that led the user to PSafe’s page on Google Play (or other similar app store), the URL subsequently accessed, the browser the user is using, the user’s IP address(es), pages/URLs visited, among other information that may be stored and retained. In the case of PSafe’s Android apps, the information may also include the “IMEIMD5” of the device, Google Advertising ID, the version of Android on the device, device information (such as model and/or device name, processor ID or serial number (MD5), manufacturer, system version, screen DPI, connection type, functions used and dates/time of use, installed applications and installation dates/time/use/removal, SIM vendor and MCC), codes that indicate the country and language of the device (or applications), and Android ID. PSafe may collect and store information about the activities of the users of the service, including (without limitation) information about their devices used to access the service (such as PC, smartphone, etc.), configuration information about their systems, metadata for their files, and the dates and times associated with their transactions and other interactions with the service.

PSafe may also collect and store information, including Personal Information, when provided by the user or a third-party that the user has authorized, including (without limitation) name, sex, and user ID on a third-party service (for example, if the user agrees to connect their Facebook and/or Google account with the PSafe service and/or product).

PSafe may collect and store Personal Information, including the user’s name and e-mail addresses (a) along with password and user name if users registers on PSafe's services; (b) if users send correspondence to PSafe (either letters or emails or through another electronic messaging tool).

If you participate in a contest, sweepstakes or other promotion, we will collect any information you provide in connection with that promotion. We also collect other types of Personal Information that you provide to us voluntarily. We may also collect Personal Information at other points in our website and services that state that Personal Information is being collected. When you participate in one of our questionnaires or customer surveys, we may collect additional Personal Information from you.

Use of Personal Information by PSafe

We use the Personal Information we collect to offer services and features that can be tailored to users’ needs, and even allows us to customize our services to make the users’ experiences with PSafe as comfortable as possible. We use the Personal Information we request as follows:

  • To develop internal studies about the interests, behaviors and demographics of users in order to better understand their needs and interests and therefore offer better services or provide related information.
  • To improve our commercial and promotional initiatives, and to analyze the pages visited and searches performed by users, in order to improve our content and product offerings and to personalize content (and its presentation) and services. To send information or messages by email about new services, display advertising or promotions, or banners of interest to our users, or news about PSafe. Should the user prefer, he may request at any time that he be excluded from the lists related to sending promotional or advertising information, by sending an e-mail to privacidade@psafe.com.
  • We may use third parties to serve ads on the site, products and services. Certain third parties may automatically collect information about your visits to our site and other websites, your IP address, your ISP, the browser you use to visit the site (but not your name, address, e-mail address or telephone number). They do this by using cookies or other technologies. Information collected may be used, among other things, to deliver advertising targeted to your interests and to better understand the usage and visitation of our site and the other sites tracked by these third parties. This policy does not apply to, and we are not responsible for, cookies or pixel tags in third party ads, and we encourage you to check the privacy policies of advertisers and/or ad services to learn about their use of cookies and other technologies. If you would like more information about this practice and to know your choices about not having this information used by these companies, click here: http://www.aboutads.info/choices.
  • We may use third party analytics services (including, but not limited to, Google Analytics) to help analyze how users use the site, products and services. Analytics services use cookies to collect information such as how often and what features of the site, products and services users use. We use this information to improve the site, products and services. The analytics services’ ability to use and share information collected by them is restricted by their terms of use and privacy policy, which you should refer to for more information about how these entities use this information, and how you can opt-out of their use of such information.
  • Winners of any commercial promotions and/or contests conducted by PSafe, or people who participate in PSafe’s loyalty programs, expressly authorize PSafe to share their names, personal data provided to PSafe to participate in the promotion, contest or program, and images (photos), in the ways it sees fit, for the purpose of advertising and/or promotion, without the right to any compensation.
  • To create anonymous information records by excluding information that makes the information personally identifiable to you. PSafe may use anonymous information for any purpose.
  • To provide users’ personal information to the legal entities that act to resolving disputes between them, such as: insurance companies, arbitration courts or other relevant courts (to resolve such disputes).

To access any services reserved exclusively for duly-registered users, such users will be provided with a personal password. The user may change it by accessing the navigation menu.

Cookies

The user and visitor to PSafe’s website accept that a monitoring system may be used, through the use of cookies. Cookies are small files that are installed on the device, with a limited duration, that help personalize the services. Additionally, cookies are used so that the user does not have to enter their password as often during a browsing session, also to confirm and provide accounting for the records, user activity, and other items related to loyalty programs (in countries where this is available) and other commercial agreements, always with the objective, regarding the installation of cookies, to benefit the user; cookies will not be used for other purposes unrelated to the operations of PSafe.

The installation, permanence and existence of cookies on the user’s device is at the user’s sole discretion, and cookies can be deleted from the device whenever the user so desires. To learn how to remove cookies from the system, it is necessary to review the Help section (or similar) of the browser.

Additionally, cookies or other similar systems installed by third parties can be found on certain pages of the PSafe website.

It is understood that this policy covers the use of cookies by this website and not the use of cookies by advertisers. PSafe does not control the use of cookies by third parties.
