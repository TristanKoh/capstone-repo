Privacy policy

Last updated: 2 February 2017

 

Thank you for using Start!

Celltick Technologies Ltd. (“Celltick”, “we”, “our” or “us”) is committed to the users’ privacy. This privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) describes how we collect, use and share your information, including any information that may personally identify you (“Personal Information”), in connection with the use of our Start application (“Start”).

By downloading or using Start, you agree to the collection, use and disclosure of your information, including Personal Information, in accordance with the terms of the Privacy Policy. Please read our Privacy Policy carefully before installing or using Start. If you do not agree to this Privacy Policy, please remove or disable Start.

This Privacy Policy forms a part of the Terms of Use, available here – http://startscreen.com/terms-conditions/ (“Terms”), and is incorporated thereto by reference, along with such other policies of which you may be notified of by us from time to time. The definitions in our Terms apply to this Privacy Policy unless stated otherwise. To learn about our privacy practices in relation to the Magazine SDK, please visit here https://startgallery.celltick.com/cdocs/site/privacy-policy-magazine.html.

 

WHAT INFORMATION WE COLLECT?

We currently do not require you to actively provide any Personal Information in order to install or access Start. However, certain features of Start may require you to submit Personal Information, as explained below.

We collect the following types of information:

  • Device information – we automatically record and collect certain information from or about your device, such as your device’s type and model, mobile device ID, language preferences, time zone, the device’s operating system, mobile carrier and internet service provider information, network connection type, mobile browsers installed on the device, hardware information, running and installed apps on your device, your device’s approximate location, your device’s IP address, GCM Registration ID, and unique device identifiers assigned to your device (a number uniquely allocated to your device by your device manufacturer). We also automatically record your Android Advertising ID (if available), for advertising and analytics purposes. The Android Advertising ID is an anonymous identifier, provided by Google Play services. By downloading or using Start, you explicitly agree that we may associate your Advertising ID with your applicable persistent device identifier. This will facilitate our ability to improve your personalized experience. If your device does not have Advertising ID, we will use other persistent identifiers.

 

  • App information, browsing history and search logs – when using Start, we may collect certain app information, including app history and usage information, information regarding downloads and installations of mobile applications and any information regarding in-app events, internet domains visited in Start, the duration of your visits and actions in these visits (such as clicks on certain items or products), your browsing history, browser and system settings, dates and time-stamps, and specific details of use of Start. In addition, certain features of Start allow you to search for content. Your depersonalized search logs may be stored locally on your device, and may be stored by our search service providers as well. We and our partners use this information to offer you relevant content, such as customized search results or tailored advertising. You may at any time delete your local search logs through the relevant settings.

 

  • Location information – we collect information about your general location (such as city and country). For example, we may use the IP address to identify your general location. This information does not tell us where your device is precisely located. This information is sent as a normal part of internet traffic. In addition, we also collect implicit location information, which allows us to infer that you are either interested in a place or that you might be at the place – this information does not actually tell us where your device is precisely located. In addition, we also collect the precise location of your device (using GPS signals, device sensors, Wi-Fi access points and cell tower ids that can be used to derive or estimate precise location, or other geo-location data), when location services have been enabled by the user.

 

  • Information we receive from our partners and other third parties – we collect information we receive from our partners, such as our analytics vendors or marketing partners, which we work with to help us improve our services and personalize your experience on Start.

 

  • Information you directly volunteer to us – to use certain features of Start, you may be asked to provide your personal information, such as your name, mobile number, email address, user-name, password and other registration information as well as age, gender, interests and other information. In addition, when you make purchases, respond to any offers, or download or use purchasable applications from us, you may be asked to submit transaction-related information (such as billing information). Further, when you contact us for support, feedbacks or questions, you may provide us with additional information. At all times, you may choose whether or not to provide such information, however, please note that if you decide not to provide such information, certain features of Start may not be enabled or may not function properly.

 

 

HOW DO WE USE THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT?

  • To improve and personalize Start – we use the information to improve and personalize your experience on Start, and to identify and authenticate your access to certain features of Start. In addition, we will use your information to provide targeted and customized content, such as interest-based ads, that enrich your experience and provide you with a personalized service, as well as enhanced services that are location-based or context-based, and to provide a superior user experience of Start.
  • Surveys and research – from time to time, we conduct surveys or test features in Start, and analyze the information we have to develop, evaluate and improve these features.
  • To communicate with you – for example, we will use your email address to respond to your queries or feedback and to keep you informed of Celltick’s latest product announcements and software updates.
  • Sweepstakes, contest and promotions – if you enter into a sweepstakes, contest or similar promotion, we may use the information you provided us to administer those programs, subject to applicable laws and regulations.
  • Direct marketing – we may use your phone number and email address to send you marketing information about products, services and offers, whether by us or by our trusted partners, which may be of relevance to you, but only as permitted by law or specifically approved by you. If you do not want to receive any more communications from us, please follow the unsubscribing instructions contained in the email, or send an email to privacy@celltick.com. Please note that unsubscribing from promotional emails will not affect our ability to send you non-promotional emails in the future.
  • Enforcement and security – we also use your information if we have a good faith belief that disclosure of such information is helpful or reasonably necessary to: (i) comply with any applicable law, regulation, legal process or governmental request; (ii) enforce our policies (including our Terms and Privacy Policy), including investigations of potential violations thereof; (iii) investigate, detect, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities or other wrongdoing, suspected fraud or security issues; (iv) to establish or exercise our rights to defend against legal claims; or (v) prevent harm to the rights, property or safety of Celltick, our users, yourself or any third party.
  • Use of Anonymous Information – we store, use or share Anonymous Information (meaning, information which does not enable identification of an individual user, such as aggregated or anonymous information) in any of the above circumstances, as well as for the purpose of providing and improving our services, aggregate statistics, advertising purposes, conduct business and marketing analysis. We may enable trusted third parties (such as ad servers) to collect Anonymous Information, by deploying certain tracking technologies within Start, including, but not limited to, for these purposes. The use of these technologies is subject to such third parties’ own privacy policies.

 

HOW WE COLLECT THE INFORMATION?

As explained above, you may either volunteer to us certain information (such as your email address), or we may automatically collect certain information, such as through the use of your mobile device system’s permissions, or through the use of cookies or similar tracking technologies. We and our trusted partners use cookies and other technologies in our related services. As is true of most applications, we gather some information automatically and store it in log files.

A “cookie” is a small piece of information that a website assigns to your device while you are viewing a website. Cookies are very helpful and can be used for various different purposes. These purposes include allowing you to navigate between pages efficiently, enable automatic activation of certain features, remembering your preferences and making the interaction between you and our services quicker and easier. Cookies are also used to help ensure that the advertisements you see are relevant to you and your interests. You can find more information about cookies at www.allaboutcookies.org. You may remove our cookies by following the instructions of your device preferences; however, if you choose to disable cookies, some features of Start may not operate properly and your online experience may be limited.

In some of our messages, we use a “click-through URL” linked to information and content. When you click on such links, they may pass through a third party web server before arriving at the destination page on Start or on one of our partners’ websites. Celltick or its trusted partners may track this click-through data to help us determine interest in particular topics, measure the effectiveness and performance of our customer communications and promotions.

To serve personalized content and ads we may use technologies that perform similar functions to cookies. Sometimes we link the identifier used for advertising on mobile applications to an advertising cookie on the same device in order to coordinate ads across your mobile apps and mobile browser. This also helps us improve the reports we give to our advertisers on the effectiveness of their campaigns.

 

WITH WHOM WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION?

We share information about you with:

  • With our service providers – we share your information with our service providers and subcontractors who assist us with our business operations (as listed above), such as for the purpose of storing the information, performing research, technical diagnostics, analytics or statistical purposes, or other business operations. Our trusted partners also assist us with personalizing your experience and presenting to you targeted advertisements. These third parties (such as advertisers, marketing affiliates, ad agencies and ad servers) use cookies, clear gifs or similar technologies to help present such advertisements, and to help track, measure and research the advertisements’ performance and effectiveness, as well as for other purposes, such as analytics and statistics.

 

We also share information with the following third parties:

Google Analytics: We may use Google Analytics cookies to collect information about visitor behavior on Start. These cookies allow us to quantify the number of users and to perform measures and statistical analysis of the utilization that the users perform of our provided services. Google Analytics stores information about what pages you visit, how long you are on Start, how you got here and what you click on.

AdMob, AdSense and DoubleClick: We may use AdMob, AdSense and DoubleClick cookies for advertising purposes. These cookies register how many times you visited Start, if the visit was a direct access, a search or a referral from other site or app, and the keywords used in the searches. Finally, these cookies are used to select and analyze the advertisements that are shown to you and their relevancy. Some advertisements can be segmented by information available about you (such as geographical location, age group or interests), while respecting your ad configuration. You can learn more about Google cookies by visiting Google’s Privacy Policy. You can also learn here how Google uses data through its partners’ sites or apps. For ad configuration, please visit here.

 

  • With our subsidiaries or in connection with a sale of our business – if a third party acquires some or all of our business or assets, we may disclose your information in connection with the sale.
  • In connection with legal proceedings – when we are under a legal obligation to do so, for example to comply with a binding order of a court, or where disclosure is necessary to exercise our or our partners’ legal rights.
  • In addition, we may disclose aggregated, anonymized information with other third parties. This information cannot be used to identify you.

 

INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

We may transfer the information we collect about you to countries other than the country where we originally collected it for the purposes of storage and processing of information and operating our services. Those countries may not have the same information protection laws as your country. However, when we transfer your information to other countries, we will protect that information as described in this Privacy Policy and take steps, where necessary, to ensure that international transfers comply with applicable laws.

 

ACCESS TO INFORMATION, RETENTION AND USER CHOICE

We wish to ensure that your Personal Information is maintained accurate. If you would like to delete or correct any of your Personal Information, you may submit a request to us by sending an email to privacy@celltick.com. Your email should include adequate details of your request and you may be further requested to provide additional details in order to authenticate your identity and thereby accommodate your requests. Please note that unless you instruct us otherwise, we retain the information we collect (including your Personal Information) for as long as needed to provide our services and to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes and enforce our policies and agreements.

You can turn off certain third party cookies for advertising purposes by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative.

To opt out of interest-based ads in apps on Android devices which make available the Android Advertising ID, please follow the instructions on your device.
