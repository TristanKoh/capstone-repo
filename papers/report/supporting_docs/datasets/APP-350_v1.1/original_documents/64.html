Privacy Policy

The usage of personal data

When you send an email to our support team, we keep the record of our communication. This includes your email, name and the conversation history. This is the only personal information, we collect, and it helps us develop, deliver, protect and improve our products, services, content and customer communication.


The usage of non-personal data

Non-personal information is anonymous data with which you cannot uniquely identify a specific individual. This includes the following information:
