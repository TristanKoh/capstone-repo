PRIVACY POLICY

Bizo Mobile respects the privacy rights of its customers and protects the personal information collected, stored and used in connection with Bizo Mobile products, services, applications and websites (the "Services"). To further this commitment, we have adopted this Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") to guide how we collect, store, and use the information you provide us.

I. SCOPE OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy applies to personal and anonymous information submitted and collected through the Services. By installing, using, registering to or otherwise accessing the Services, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this Privacy Policy, please do not install, use, register to or otherwise access the Services. Bizo Mobile reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at reasonable times, so please review it frequently. Your continued used of the Services will signify your acceptance to the changes to this Privacy Policy.

II. INFORMATION COLLECTED

1) We use technologies like cookies (small files stored by your browser), web beacons, or unique device identifiers to anonymously identify your computer or device so we can deliver a better experience. Our systems also log information like your browser, operating system and IP address, device information.
2) We collect information about how you use our apps, your results (in case of a game), how often you use a specific feature.
We do not knowingly contact or collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly obtain parental consent or remove the information.
3) In serving you, we may use or store your precise geographic location, if you give us permission to do so.
4) If you use Facebook or Google Account to register into our app, we collect only your name, e-mail addres and store it only for registering or loging in purposes. In case of Facebook we may collect your friends list or other information provided by Facebook that you agree to give to us.
5) In case of applications which purpose is to process images, videos, sound, we don't collect your recorded video, image or sound anywhere on our servers. All files recorded by you are stored on your device's memory card. We don't share it to any third party company, or anywhere in the Internet.

III. USE OF INFORMATION COLLECTED


1) Ad companies may use and collect anonymous data about your interests to customize content and advertising here and in other sites and applications. Interest and location data may be linked to your device, but is not linked to your identity. Ad companies that we cooperate with, and which may collect anonymous data: admob.com facebook.com applovin.com
2) We don't share your personal data such as name, e-mail or other (collected from Google Account or Facebook) to any third party.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policies, please contact us:
contact@bizomobile.com
