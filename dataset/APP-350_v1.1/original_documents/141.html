If you have questions or concerns about our privacy policy or practices, please contact us at privacy@appturbo.it.

Information collection

We collects information as described below. AppTurbo's primary goals in collecting and using information is to provide services to you, improve our service, contact you, conduct research and create reports for internal use. We store information on servers located in the United States via Amazon Web Services and may store information on servers and equipment in other countries.

Push notifications

This application may send push notifications to you.
You may at any time opt-out from receiving these types of communications by turning them off at the device level through your settings.

Facebook Authentification

By registering or authenticating, you allow us to identify you and give you access to dedicated services.
Facebook Authentication is a registration and authentication service provided by Facebook, Inc. and is connected to the Facebook social network.
We are able to access some data, stored by Facebook, for registration or identification purposes.
In addition, we may collect and record information through Facebook in accordance with the policies and terms of Facebook. For more information, you can access to their Privacy Policy here.
If you want us to delete the data we receive from Facebook about you, please contact us at privacy@appturbo.it.

Data collection

Whenever you use our application, we collect data about all of your interactions with the application via server log files. This information may be associated with your advertising Id (Google Advertising Id) for the purpose of providing you our services and improving them. This data is stored within Amazon Web Services.

Location-based interaction

We may collect your location based information for the purpose of providing you location-based services. We will not share this information with any third party. The geographic location of the User is determined in a manner that isn't continuous. Please note, the application may not work properly if we are unable to tell where you are coming from (Country specific).

Third party Services

Our services may contain third party tracking tools from our service providers, that include Google Analytics, Facebook Analytics for Apps and Crashlytics. Such third parties may use cookies, APIs, and SDKs in our services to enable them to collect and analyze user information on our behalf. The third parties may have access to information such as your device identifier, MAC address, IMEI, locale (specific location where a given language is spoken), geo-location information, and IP address for the purpose of providing their services under their respective privacy policies. Our privacy policy does not cover the use of tracking tools from third parties. We do not have access or control over these third parties.
For operation and maintenance purposes, this application and any third party services may collect files that record interaction with this Application (System logs) or use for this purpose other personal data (such as IP Address).

Social media

Our application includes social media features, such as the Facebook Like button and widgets, such as the Share button. These Features may collect your IP address and may set a cookie to enable the feature to function properly. Social media features and widgets are either hosted by a third party or hosted directly on our application. Your interactions with these features are governed by the privacy policy of the company providing it.

Ad network

We may feature advertising within our service. The advertisers may collect and use information about you, such as your service session activity, or your Google advertising ID. They may use this information to provide advertisements of interest to you.

Personalized Advertising

This application may use location data and personal information for ad serving purposes and don't share any of these data to third party services.
