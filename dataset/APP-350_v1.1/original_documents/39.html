Privacy

Beijing Elex Technology Co., Ltd. (“ELEX”) see it as our responsibility to set examples and help establish the standards for privacy on the Internet. During registration, each service asks the user to provide different types and amounts of personal information. ELEX intends through the use of such information in good faith to deliver better services.

ELEX is dedicated to protecting the privacy rights of its online users (“users” or “user”). This Online Privacy Policy (“Privacy Policy”) has been crafted to inform users as to the ways ELEX collects, stores, uses, and manages the information users provide during the use of any ELEX game or application (“Service”). Please note that the scope of this Privacy Policy is only applicable to information collected from ELEX through the use of the Service.

By using the Service, users are agreeing to the collection and use of their personal information as outlined in this Privacy Policy. ELEX may amend the Privacy Policy from time to time, and ELEX encourages users to consult the Privacy Policy regularly for changes.

Personal Information and Its Use

ELEX gathers two types of information about users: (1) Non-personally identifiable information: When visitors come to our web site, we collect and aggregate information indicating, among other things, which pages were visited, the order in which they were visited, and which hyperlinks were "clicked." Collecting such information involves the logging of IP addresses, operating system and browser software etc. used by each visitor to the ELEX web site. Although such information is not personally identifiable, we can determine from the IP address a visitor's Internet Service Provider and the geographic location of his or her point of connectivity. The non-personally identifiable information we collect helps us identify the most popular areas of our web site and determine the effectiveness of our promotional activities. In addition, we may disclose some of this information to advertisers to inform them of the number of people who have seen and/or clicked through their advertisements. (2) Personally identifiable information: In certain areas of the ELEX web site -for example, while registering for the game service a user may be asked to provide his/her full name, nick name, province, gender, hobbies, age and interests etc.

ELEX collects this personal information primarily to make using the ELEX Web site easier and more satisfying for its users. The goal of ELEX is to provide all Internet users with an exciting, entertaining, online experience.

All users should be aware that, when they disclose personally identifiable information in a bulletin board or other forum, the information may be collected and used by others to send that person unsolicited email from other parties outside ELEX. In the event that you encounter any ELEX user who is improperly collecting or using information about you or other users, please contact Privacy@ ELEX-tech.com.

Information Disclosure

ELEX will not disclose your personally identifiable information except:

a) with your prior authorization or consent;

b) in an emergency to protect rights and interests of ELEX and its users, public security and public interests;

c) as required by law or valid order by a court or other governmental body;

d) to enforce applicable terms of ELEX services or any statements of ELEX, or in other situation which ELEX may deem necessary.

Information Collecting during Co-operation with Third Parties

The information that a user shares with ELEX is collected, stored, and handled carefully by ELEX. However, ELEX may share such information during co-operation with third parties to offer some of its services in conjunction with such third parties. ELEX will urge such third parties to handle the said information in compliance with this Privacy Policy.

Through good co-operation or even alliance with other reputable organizations of goodwill, ELEX has been dedicated to providing the best internet service to its users.

Minors' Information and Its Use

ELEX suggests that minors shall not access internet without prior written consent of their parents or other legal guardians. ELEX will protect relevant information of minors as required by laws and regulations.

Cookies

A cookie is a small data file that ELEX transfers to users’ computer’s hard disk, generally to quickly identify a user’s computer and to “remember” things about the user’s visit, such as the user’s preferences or a user’s name and password. The Service sends cookies to users’ computers when users access or view content of an ELEX game. Information contained in a cookie may be linked to users’ personal information for purposes such as improving the quality of our service, tailoring recommendations to users’ interests, and making the Service easier to use. Users can disable cookies at any time, although users may not be able to access or use features of the Service.

Provision of Information

ELEX users always have a choice whether or not to disclose information. Certain information is required for registration of our services, but most other requested information is optional. It is a top priority at ELEX to make its user feel comfortable and in control.

ELEX offers its users numerous online interactive activities. To facilitate participation of the user in interactive activities and issue of prizes from such activities, ELEX usually requires the applicant to fill out forms pertaining to his/her personal interests in addition to contact information (i.e. mailing address and phone number). This information is used by ELEX to compile aggregate data on its users. If the user does not want to supply the requested information and partake in the interactive activities, it is completely at the user's discretion.

Links

The ELEX web site contains links to some other web sites. ELEX is not responsible for the content or the privacy policy or practices of any other web sites or for the actions of the companies responsible for those web sites. Update and Publicity of Information

ELEX strongly encourages its users to update and edit their personal information and keep it current. A ELEX community user can easily access and correct his/her personal information at any time. Users can edit and delete their personal information at their discretion.

Please keep in mind that whenever you voluntarily disclose personal information online through certain channel ( for example on message boards, through email, or in chat areas ), that information can be collected and used by others. ELEX will take no responsibility whatsoever caused by reasons said above. Since disclosure of personal information through certain channel may lead to disclosure of your personal information, we remind you to use caution before such disclosing.

Ultimately, you are solely responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your passwords and/or any account information. Please be careful and responsible whenever you're online.
